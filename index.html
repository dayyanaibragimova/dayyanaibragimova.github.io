About Me

My name is Dayyana Ibragimova, and I am an incoming student in the Master in Professional Accounting Program at the University of Texas at Austin. In May 2020, I graduated Magna Cum Laude from Illinois State University with a double major in Finance and Spanish, and a minor in International Business. Outside of my studies, I enjoy traveling, learning new languages, and spending time with family and friends. I also really enjoy to read, learn, and better myself. I am an advocate for female empowerment, and I strive to be a #girlboss in all areas of life.
