Why I Wrote This

While the purpose of my website is to share my knowledge and advice on certain topics, I thought that is was equally as important to share more about who I am as a person. Get to know a little more about me through these 10 facts.

1.I was born in Uzbekistan.

I am originally from Uzbekistan and moved to the United States when I was 11 years old. Growing up in a predominately Muslim country, I was always told that women should be inferior to men, and should only have the purpose of getting married and taking care of home and children. However, that never seemed to resonate with me. As a litle girl, I always looked up to my uncle, who worked internationally. I wanted to study and have a very successful, international, career myself. Coming to the United States has given me opportunities to have equal rights and freedom to choose whichever path I wanted to as a woman, and I am so excited for the opportunity to have an exciting career just like I have always wanted.

2. I am trilingual.

Because I grew up in Uzbekistan, Russian was my first language. I learned English after coming to the States and later started studying Spanish in high school. Like I said, I have always dreamed of having an international career, so learning more languages was a must. I also have a great passion for learning about other cultures and traveling.

3.I have been to 17 countries.

My uncle, who traveled a lot when I was little, would always bring me little gifts from different countries - I still remember being one of a few girls in Uzbekistan with Barbies from France! He would tell me about how different the culture was in those countries. Since then, I have always had a curiosity for travel. I spend any extra money for my travels within the United States and abroad, and even studied abroad in Granada, Spain for a semester during my Bachelor's.

4.I am a sucker for romantic comedies.

Name a romantic comedy, and I have most likely seen it - twice. La La Land is my all-time favorite movie, and I am also a huge fan of the Nicholas Sparks movies.

5.I worked in customer service for over 5 years.

I got a job in a gelato shop as soon I turned 16. Since then, I have also worked as a server all through high-school and during breaks in college. I believe that customer service is one of the toughest jobs but it is also super rewarding. I learned a lot of life lessons through these jobs as a teenager and young adult and am grateful for every moment.

6.I love to read.

While I do not read very much during the school year, I love reading during the summer and whenever I have free time. My favorite type of books are self improvement books, and I will be writing about my recommendations in a later post!
